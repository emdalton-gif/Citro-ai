Common questions

What exactly is a Citro audit?
Citro simulates the way your buyers actually use AI — building synthetic buyer personas for your industry and running blind queries across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, Copilot, Meta AI, and Grok. It scores how often your brand appears versus competitors and produces a prioritized action plan to improve your position. Results deliver in under 5 minutes, no calls or waiting required.
How does the free trial work?
Professional starts with a 7-day free trial. You add a card but are not charged until day 8, and you can cancel anytime before then from your dashboard. During the trial you get the full product: unlimited re-runs, score tracking over time, and report history, so you see exactly where you stand before your first bill.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Yes. Cancel anytime from your account dashboard — no phone calls, no hoops. You keep access through the end of your billing period.
What if the report isn't useful?
Email us within 7 days of delivery and we'll refund you in full, no questions asked. We're confident the report will show you something actionable — but if it doesn't, you shouldn't pay for it.
Who is this built for?
Citro is built for B2B companies whose buyers use AI to research options before making a purchase — professional services, SaaS, consulting, healthcare, and similar industries. If your buyers are asking AI "who should I call for X" and you're not sure whether your name comes up, Citro answers that question directly.
