The B2B AI problem

The buying committee is consulting AI before they consult you.

The B2B buying cycle has always been complex. Multiple stakeholders, long evaluation periods, heavy research phases. What's changed is where that research begins.

A VP of Marketing evaluating GTM platforms doesn't open a browser and search Google. They open ChatGPT or Perplexity and ask a question they'd previously have asked a trusted colleague. The AI builds a shortlist. Sometimes it adds context. Sometimes it compares vendors.

If your brand isn't appearing in those answers — or appearing unfavorably — you're losing deals before the first call happens. And you won't see it in your pipeline data until months later.

Citro makes the invisible visible. It runs your actual buyers' actual questions through every major AI platform and shows you exactly where you stand, why, and what to do about it.

How it works

From onboarding to ongoing intelligence in three steps.

No long implementation timelines. No new tooling to deploy. The platform runs on your business context and surfaces findings immediately.

01 — Profile

Build your buyer persona library

We work with your team to define the three to five buyer personas that represent your actual buying committee — the titles, goals, pain points, and search behaviors of the people who evaluate solutions like yours. These become the foundation for every audit.

02 — Simulate

Run structured buyer simulations

Citro sends each persona's queries — the real questions B2B buyers ask AI platforms during vendor research — across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google Gemini, Claude, Microsoft Copilot, Grok, and Meta AI. Every response is captured, analyzed, and scored.

03 — Act

Receive a scored report with a concrete roadmap

Each audit produces a presence score, a competitive benchmark, and a prioritized action plan. RootPartners clients additionally receive a quarterly strategy session to review trend movement and refine the execution roadmap with the consulting team.
